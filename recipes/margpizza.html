Puff Pastry Margherita Pizza

Description

This Italian puff pastry pizza makes for a quick and delicious lunch or dinner. The results are a light, flaky crust. Feel free to modify with your favorite pizza toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 sheets frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 (14.5 ounce can) whole peeled tomatoes, drained
  • 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 pinch sea salt
  • 6 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, chopped
  • 1/4 cup shredded Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • 10 basil leaves, torn
